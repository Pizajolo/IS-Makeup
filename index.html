01 — About

A decade of tender, lasting bridal beauty.

Trained in Lisbon, working across Portugal's wedding coast and abroad — from intimate Sintra ceremonies to destination weddings in the Mediterranean.

Makeup, to me, is a quiet act of care. I start with the skin you already love and add only what makes it sing — a softer cheek, a steadier brow, eyes that hold tears and still photograph beautifully at midnight.

Ten years in, I've worked with brides from Porto to Puglia, with photographers, planners, and couture ateliers who trust me to be calm, prepared, and unhurried. I bring a small, considered kit, a curated palette, and a steady hand.

Above all, I want my brides to feel exactly like themselves — only on the most luminous day of their lives.

Inés Santiago Founder & Artist · I.S Makeup
